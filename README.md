# online food delivery databse system
 

## Overview of the project

### Project: Online Food Delivery Database System

## Objective
The objective of this project is to develop a structured and efficient database system that simplifies food ordering, restaurant management, delivery tracking, and customer experience enhancement. This system helps ensure smooth order processing, timely deliveries, and intelligent data-driven decision-making.

### Key Activities
- **Restaurant Management**: Store and manage restaurant details, menu items, prices, availability, and preparation time.
- **Customer Management**: Maintain customer profiles, delivery addresses, contact details, and order histories.
- **Order Processing**: Record food orders, payment statuses, delivery instructions, and timestamps.
- **Delivery Tracking**: Track delivery partners, assign orders, monitor delivery time, and update delivery completion status.
- **Payment & Billing**: Manage payment modes, transaction details, bills, and invoices.
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Generate reports on popular items, sales trends, peak hours, and customer behavior.

### Technologies Used
- **MySQL**: Used for creating tables, managing relational data, executing queries, joins, stored procedures, and handling order records.
- **Oracle**: Used for advanced query optimization, triggers, indexing, and handling large-scale structured data.

### Key Insights
- **Customer Insights**: Analyze customer order patterns, frequently ordered dishes, and total spend.
- **Restaurant Insights**: Identify best-selling items and high-performing restaurants.
- **Order & Delivery Analysis**: Track delivery time efficiency, delays, and optimize delivery assignment.
- **Sales Analytics**: Monitor daily, weekly, and monthly sales; identify peak order hours.
- **Operational Efficiency**: Automate order confirmations, reminders, delivery notifications, and minimize human errors.
